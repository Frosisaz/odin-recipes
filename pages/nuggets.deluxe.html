Nuggets deluxe

This recipe is just a clasic, fast and easy to make and even faster to eat

Ingredinets

  • one pack of frozen chicken nuggets
  • half a pack of frozen french fries
  • 3-4 spoons of honey
  • 3-4 spoons of mustard

Cooking Instructions

  1. take the pack of frozen chicken nuggets, open it and spread the nuggets on aluminium foil
  2. open the pack of frozen french fries and put half of it on the same foil next to the nuggets
  3. put them in te oven for 15-20 minutes
  4. while you are waiting, put the mustard and honey in a sauce bowl and mix them till you get a homogenous mixture
